Current Newsletter

We are very proud to let members and friends of the Michaux St. James Foundation (MSJF) know that together, we have fully completed ALL of the historical restoration work on the beautiful Sallie W. Michaux House (circa 1868).

How appropriate an accomplishment as the Sallie Michaux House turns 150 years old in the next year.

There are many people who deserve lots of thanks for this huge accomplishment, but first and foremost we must thank Carson Tucker who loaned and donated significant funds to allow the MSJ Foundation to purchase the home in 2009.
