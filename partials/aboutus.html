Our Mission

The purpose of the Michaux-St. James Foundation is to promote for the benefit of the general public the preservation and improvement of historical, cultural and natural resources principally but not limited to Northern Powhatan County, specifically the Michaux and Fine Creek Areas. The 501 C Corporation is organized exclusively for educational, charitable, scientific, conservation, historical and literary purposes.

Our People

Over the past decade, over 200 neighbors and community members have donated their time and made charitable donations to help with the restoration and preservation of numerous historic Powhatan County properties. These generous people make up the backbone of our organization and are true heroes in our community
