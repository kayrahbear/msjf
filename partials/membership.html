Get Involved

The Michaux-St. James Foundation has a high level of civic engagement in creating a strong and caring community. Each new member adds strength to our organization, and together we provide a powerful collective voice on behalf of historic and environmental preservation. Our success depends on the support and involvement of many people and organizations. Your membership contribution or charitable gift will be used to continue and increase our historic preservation programs and community services in the Powhatan region.

Family memberships are only $25 annually. You may complete the form below if you would like to pay online by credit or debit card. If you would prefer to print your application and mail it to us along with your check: click here

Support Our Efforts:
  • Revitalize and stand guard over architectural landmarks of Powhatan County.
  • Provide a public meeting place via the St. James Community Center.
  • Provide assistance to community organizations and neighborhood groups.
  • Create tours, publications, and educational programs featuring the history and architecture of the Powhatan region.
  • Contribute to the economy of central Virginia by developing resources based on our architectural and cultural heritage and the good use of our rivers.
  • Evaluate future development proposals as an advocate for the preservation of the Powhatan region's special character.
  • Continue a well-managed, responsive, and creative membership organization with the ability to implement these goals on a long-range basis.
Membership Benefits:
  • A free subscription to our quarterly newsletter featuring current projects, important issues and events.
  • The opportunity to work with MSJF to realize benefits for you and your family.
  • News updates plus invitations to all MSJF sponsored events and activities.
  • Opportunities to hold your own special events at the St. James Community Center.
  • Free access to the MSJF archives, images, and reference materials.
  • The satisfaction of knowing that you are supporting one of the region's most innovative and community-focused organizations.
  • Many rewarding volunteer opportunities.
